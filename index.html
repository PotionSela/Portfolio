Early Beginnings:

Gisela Mata, a native of San Francisco, spent her formative years in the vibrant heart of the city until the age of 8. At that point, her family embarked on a journey to Maine, seeking a new beginning and fresh opportunities. During her summers, Gisela immersed herself in the enchanting wilderness of Augusta, Maine, at an all-girls sleep-away camp known as Camp Chewonki. Over six memorable summers, from the age of 11 to 17, she delved into the beauty of nature and honed her skills for living in the wild. Her time at Camp Chewonki not only deepened her appreciation for the preciousness of nature but also fostered lifelong friendships that continue to enrich her journey.

Artistic Background:

Gisela grew up with an ardent passion for art and creative design. She was more than willing to dedicate countless hours to her artistic endeavors, whether it was through film, pottery, paintings, drawings, or various forms of artistic expression. As her artistic journey evolved, she found herself drawn to the captivating world of comics, where she explored both writing and illustrating. This newfound avenue ignited a deep-seated love for storytelling through visuals. It was in this creative milieu that "A Story For Jen" came to life, her first comic, portraying a short but vivid tale of a group of friends and their adventures after school. Gisela's artistic talents continue to be a source of inspiration and a means to share her unique narratives with the world.

After College:

After successfully completing her initial two years in community college, Gisela embarked on a path of exploration to chart her educational and professional journey. While the allure of attending an art university piqued her interest, she decided to take a broader route, driven by her desire to delve into a multitude of disciplines. This pivot led her into the dynamic world of customer service, where she delved into the intricate nuances of consumer behavior, shopping preferences, and the art of making everyday experiences effortless for customers. Her time in customer service proved to be an invaluable experience, enabling her to interact adeptly with individuals from all walks of life and ensuring that they departed with a smile, highlighting her commitment to customer satisfaction.

Future:

Having dedicated several years to unravel the intricacies of customer service and understanding how to best cater to the public's needs, Gisela had an epiphany. She recognized the potential to harmoniously blend her artistic background and her wealth of experience in customer service to forge a career path in software development, with a specific emphasis on UX/UI design. Acknowledging that mastering this new skill would require time and practice, Gisela wholeheartedly embraced the coding community, where she saw limitless opportunities for growth. Her vision for the future is rooted in continuous improvement, as she wholeheartedly commits to honing her knowledge and skills through persistent practice and unwavering dedication to her craft. This journey into the world of software development is where she envisions her true calling and a thriving career.
